IAAI International Conference – 2024

Innovation Aviation & Aerospace Industry - International Conference 2024 Aerospace and Aviation Symposium June 2024, 10th – 12th at Bangkok, Thailand

Welcome from the Chairs

On behalf of the Innovation Aviation & Aerospace Industry - International Conference 2021 Organizing Committee, we are pleased to announce our second Innovation Aviation & Aerospace Industry - International Conference 2024. The conference is organized by the International Academy of Aviation Industry (IAAI), King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang (KMITL) with special partner, and will be held as a three-day event in Bangkok, Thailand, from June 10th‒12th, 2024. The conference has a main focus on "Aerospace and Aviation Symposium."

IAAI International Conference aims to become the leading annual conference in fields related to the aviation and aerospace industries. This conference focuses on innovations in aviation and aerospace by fostering knowledge and new ideas for research work within the community, speakers, plenary speeches, young researchers and practitioners. The conference also seeks to provide forums, oral presentations, technical workshops, and scientific sessions. We invite researchers, aerospace and aviation industry representatives, and others with an interest in the impacts of the aerospace and aviation industry to join us for what will be an exciting event.

We hope to see you in the vibrant and exciting city of Bangkok in June 2024.

    Conference Venue

    Bangkok, Thailand

    Bangkok is the capital and largest city of Thailand. It is located in the central part of the country, along the Chao Phraya River. Bangkok serves as the economic, cultural, and political center of Thailand and is one of the most vibrant and bustling cities in Southeast Asia. Bangkok is renowned for its stunning temples, such as Wat Arun, Wat Phra Kaew (Temple of the Emerald Buddha), and Wat Pho (Temple of the Reclining Buddha). The city is also famous for its ornate palaces, including the Grand Palace and the nearby Dusit Palace. Other popular attractions include the bustling street markets, floating markets, vibrant nightlife, and modern shopping malls. Bangkok attracts millions of tourists every year who come to explore its vibrant street life, cultural landmarks, and famous hospitality. The city serves as a gateway to other popular tourist destinations in Thailand, such as Ayutthaya, Pattaya, and the islands in the Gulf of Thailand.
