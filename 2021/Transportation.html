IAAI International Conference – 2021

Innovation Aviation & Aerospace Industry - International Conference 2021 Astronomical and Aerospace Symposium June 2021, 28th – 30th at Princess Sirindhorn AstroPark, Chiang Mai, Thailand

By air( 1 hour)

The airport has daily flights to Bangkok via Nok Air, Thai Vietjet Air, Thai Lion Air and Thai AirAsia which operate multiple daily flights between Bangkok (Don Mueang, DMK) and Chiang Mai Airport (CNX). Bangkok has two airports - Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) and Don Mueang Airport (DMK). Traveling between them can be done in several ways including shuttle bus service BKK to DMK / DMK to BKK.

Transportation between Bangkok Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) and Don Muang Airport (DMK)
- There is a free shuttle bus service in between the two Bangkok airports.
- This is a non-stop service which runs from 5 AM to Midnight.
- It takes the expressway and the travelling time is roughly 1-3 hours.
- You need to show your air ticket and passport prior to boarding the shuttle bus.
- From 05.00 to 08.00 / 11.30 to 16.00 / 19.30 to Midnight buses depart every 30 minutes.
- From 08.12 to 11.00 and 16.12 to 19.00 buses depart every 12 minutes.
- At Suvarnabhumi airport the free shuttle can be boarded on the 2nd floor between Gate 2 and 3.
- At Don Muang airport the free shuttle can be boarded outside the International Terminal 1 on the 1st floor between Gate 4 and 5.
- For more information please contact Suvarnabhumi Airport call centre by dialling 1722 or Don Muang call centre by dialling 0-2535-1111
*** Please make a confirmation with staffs at the airport for other/changing information.***

BY TRAIN (11-14 hours)

Trains from Bangkok to Chiang Mai and Chiang Mai to Bangkok are served daily, five times a day. Different times will have different train types, ticket prices, and ticket types are subject to change. The train will leave from Hua Lamphong Railway Station. And will stop at Don Mueang Railway Station to pick up more passengers. Both train stations are located in the Bangkok district. Please find more information on the website.
***Time and fares might change depending on the season, subject to the operator***
