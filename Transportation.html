Innovation Aviation & Aerospace Industry - International Conference 2020 January 2020, 13th – 17th at Chumphon, Thailand

Transportation

By air(estimated 1 hour)

The city's airport, Chumphon Airport (IATA: CJM, ICAO: VTSE) is 30 km north in Pathio District. The airport has daily flights to Bangkok. Nok Air and Thai AirAsia operate multiple daily flights between Bangkok (Don Mueang, DMK) and Chumphon Airport (CJM). Bangkok has two airports - Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) and Don Mueang Airport (DMK). Traveling between them can be done in several ways including shuttle bus service BKK to DMK / DMK to BKK.

Transportation between Bangkok Suvarnabhumi airport (BKK) and Don Muang airport (DMK)
- There is a free shuttle bus service in between the two Bangkok airports.
- This is a non-stop service which runs from 5 AM to Midnight.
- It takes the expressway and the travelling time is roughly 1-3 hours.
- You need to show your air ticket and passport prior to boarding the shuttle bus.
- From 05.00 to 08.00 / 11.30 to 16.00 / 19.30 to Midnight buses depart every 30 minutes.
- From 08.12 to 11.00 and 16.12 to 19.00 buses depart every 12 minutes.
- At Suvarnabhumi airport the free shuttle can be boarded on the 2nd floor between Gate 2 and 3.
- At Don Muang airport the free shuttle can be boarded outside the International Terminal 1 on the 1st floor between Gate 4 and 5.
- For more information please contact Suvarnabhumi Airport call centre by dialling 1722 or Don Muang call centre by dialling 0-2535-1111
*** Please make a confirmation with staffs at the airport for other/changing information.***

By car (6-8 hours)

From Bangkok it is possible to drive to Chumphon via highway No. 4 or highway No. 35 to highway No.4 through Petchaburi and Prachuap Khiri Khan Provinces. At Chumphon junction turn left along highway No. 4001 for the final 8 kilometers to Chumphon town.

By Train (7-10 hours)

Train from Bangkok to Chumphon is one of the popular train routes in Thailand. Many locals and tourists take Bangkok to Chumphon train because Chumphon is the gateway to one of the most popular islands in Thailand named Koh Tao.

Taking train from Bangkok to Chumphon is one of the more popular ways to get to Chumphon from Bangkok besides taking bus. This is because both options offer a cheaper price compared to flight tickets. Additionally, taking Bangkok to Chumphon train offers extra comfort depending on the type of train ticket that you booked. On top of that, Chumphon Airport is located far away from the pier to get to Koh Tao. Therefore, taking train is a more convenient way to go to Chumphon from Bangkok.
